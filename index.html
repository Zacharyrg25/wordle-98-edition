Wordle 98

Wordle 98 is a remake of the popular New York Times' Wordle game, but designed with a retro Windows 98 aesthetic.

In this game, you get 6 tries to guess a target word. After each guess, the color of the tile will change to indicate how close your guess is to the target word.

Take the guess below for exapmle.

H
E
L
L
O

H is in the correct spot, E is in the target word but in the wrong spot, and L and O are not in the target word at all.

X

This button doesn't really do anything. Just wanted to stay consistent with the Windows 98 aesthetic.
